5 Things Not to Do on Hinge: Tips for Finding Love Online

Online dating can be a great way to meet new people, but it can also be a minefield of pitfalls and faux pas. Hinge is a popular dating app, but it's important to know what not to do when using it. Below, you'll find five things not to do when it comes to your photos and five things to avoid when writing your prompts.

Things Not to Do With Your Photos

1. Ditch The Guessing Game: According to Hinge, you want to pick photos that make it easy to see what you look like. That means no sunglasses, no hats, and no group shots.

2. No Gym Photos Allowed: You may be proud of your six-pack abs, but Hinge users don't want to see them. Save the gym selfies for Instagram.

3. No Smokers Allowed: If you're a smoker, you may want to avoid posting photos of yourself with a cigarette or cigar. Non-smokers may be turned off by this.

4. Don't Be A Class Clown: Hinge is not the place for funny, jokey photos. Save those for your social media accounts.

5. Don't Post Old Photos: It's important to post recent photos that accurately reflect what you look like now. Don't post photos from years ago.

Things Not to Do With Your Prompts

1. Don't Be Too Vague: When writing your prompts, be sure to be specific. Vague prompts don't give potential matches enough information to go on.

2. Don't Be Too Long-Winded: Keep your prompts short and sweet. No one wants to read a novel when they're trying to get to know you.

3. Don't Be Too Negative: It's important to be honest in your prompts, but don't be too negative. Avoid complaining about past relationships or other negative topics.

4. Don't Be Too Sexual: Hinge is not the place for sexual innuendos or references. Keep it clean and classy.

5. Don't Use Repetitive Prompts: Hinge allows you to use the same prompts for multiple photos, but don't use the same prompt for every photo. Mix it up and be creative.

By following these tips, you can make sure you're using Hinge in the best way possible. Good luck!
