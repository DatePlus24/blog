How do you restart a text conversation with a guy?


Are you looking to restart a conversation with a guy you haven't talked to in a while? Whether you’re looking to reconnect with an old flame or just get back in touch with a friend, there are some simple tips and tricks to help you restart a conversation with a guy.

Acknowledge that it's been awhile
When restarting a conversation with a guy, it’s important to acknowledge that it’s been awhile since you’ve talked. This helps to break the ice and show that you’re interested in getting back in touch. Try something like, “Hey, it’s been a while since we’ve talked. How have you been?”

Follow up on previous topics
If you’ve talked to the guy before, it’s a good idea to follow up on previous topics. This helps to show that you remember what you talked about and that you’re interested in continuing the conversation. Try something like, “I remember you were telling me about your new job. How’s it going?”

Send an intriguing message
If you’re looking to start a conversation with a guy you haven’t talked to before, try sending an intriguing message. Ask him a question that’s open-ended and encourages him to respond. This could be something like, “What’s the craziest thing you’ve done this year?”

Compliment him
Compliments are a great way to start a conversation with a guy. Try to give him a genuine compliment that’s related to something he’s done recently. This could be something like, “I saw your post about your new project. You’re so talented!”

Ask for his opinion
Asking for someone’s opinion is a great way to start a conversation. Ask him a question that’s related to something he’s interested in. This could be something like, “What do you think about the new movie that just came out?”

Offer a fun assumption about him
Offering a fun assumption about the guy is a great way to start a conversation. Try to make an assumption that’s related to something he’s interested in. This could be something like, “I bet you’re a great cook.”

Send him a meme
Memes are a great way to start a conversation with a guy. Try to find a meme that’s related to something he’s interested in. This could be something like, “I saw this meme and thought of you.”

Pretend to message him by accident
If you’re feeling a bit shy, you can try pretending to message him by accident. This could be something like, “Oops, wrong person. Hey, how’s it going?”

Conclusion
Restarting a conversation with a guy doesn’t have to be difficult. By following these simple tips and tricks, you can easily get back in touch with someone you haven’t talked to in a while. Acknowledge that it’s been awhile, follow up on previous topics, send an intriguing message, compliment him, ask for his opinion, offer a fun assumption about him, send him a meme, or pretend to message him by accident. With these tips, you’ll be able to restart a conversation with a guy in no time.
