How long should you wait to go on a date with someone you met online?


Meeting someone online can be exciting and nerve-wracking at the same time. You want to make sure you take the right steps to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience. One of the most important questions to consider is how long you should wait to go on a date with someone you met online.

Going on a date with someone you met online is when two people who initially connected through an online platform, such as a dating website or social media, decide to meet in person.

1. Try to Meet in Person Within 17-23 Days:

Research suggests that it is best to wait 17-23 days before meeting someone you met online in person. This gives you time to get to know each other better and build a connection before meeting in person. It also gives you time to make sure the person is who they say they are and that they are trustworthy.

2. Meet Up More Quickly if There's a Strong Connection:

If you and the person you met online have an instant connection, it may be okay to meet up sooner than 17-23 days. However, it is still important to make sure the person is who they say they are and that they are trustworthy.

3. Ask Open-Ended Questions in Your First Online Conversation:

When you first start talking to someone you met online, it is important to ask open-ended questions. This will help you get to know the person better and find out if you have anything in common.

4. Have Several In-Depth Conversations Online Before Meeting:

Before meeting someone you met online in person, it is important to have several in-depth conversations. This will help you get to know the person better and make sure you are both on the same page.

Brief Conclusion:

Meeting someone you met online can be a great experience, but it is important to take the right steps to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience. It is best to wait 17-23 days before meeting in person, but if there is a strong connection, it may be okay to meet up sooner. Make sure to ask open-ended questions and have several in-depth conversations online before meeting in person.
