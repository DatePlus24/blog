How Much Does Plenty of Fish Cost?


Plenty of Fish (POF) is one of the most popular online dating sites in the world. It has a large user base and offers a variety of features, making it a great choice for those looking for love. But how much does POF cost?

Monthly Plans

POF offers several different plans, ranging from a single month to an eight-month commitment. The prices vary depending on the length of the plan. The single month plan starts at $19.99, while the eight-month plan is as low as $9.99 per month.

Additional Features

In addition to the basic plan, POF also offers several additional features. These include the ability to see who has viewed your profile, access to advanced search filters, and the ability to send and receive unlimited messages. These features come at an additional cost, but can be a great way to increase your chances of finding a match.

Free Features

POF also offers several free features, including the ability to create a profile, upload photos, and search for other users. You can also use the site's "Meet Me" feature, which allows you to browse through potential matches and send them a message.

Bottom Line

POF is a great choice for those looking for love. The basic plan starts at $19.99 for a single month, or as low as $9.99 per month for an eight-month commitment. Additional features are available at an additional cost, but the site also offers several free features.
