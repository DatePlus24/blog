How to Get on Raya: The Exclusive Dating App

Raya is an exclusive dating app that is known for its celebrity user base and high standards for admission. If you're curious about how to join this elite community, here's everything you need to know.

Invitation Only

Unlike most dating apps, you can't just download Raya and create a profile. Instead, you must be invited by a current member who can vouch for you. This means that you'll need to know someone who is already on the app to have a chance of being admitted. The app does not reveal how many members are currently using it.

High Standards

Even if you do receive an invitation, that doesn't guarantee admission. Raya has very high standards for its members, and the application process is rigorous. The app looks for people who are creative, interesting, and accomplished in their fields. This means that having a large following on social media or being a celebrity in your own right may not be enough to get you in.

Application Process

Once you receive an invitation, you'll need to fill out an application that asks for basic information like your name, age, and location, as well as more detailed questions about your career and interests. You'll also need to provide a few photos that show off your personality and style. The application fee is $7.99 and there is a monthly membership fee.

Approval Process

After you submit your application, a panel of anonymous members reviews it and decides whether or not to admit you. Raya does not disclose the criteria it uses to make these decisions, but the app is known for being very selective. It's not uncommon for people to be rejected, even if they were invited by a current member.

Celebrity User Base

One of the things that sets Raya apart is its celebrity user base. Many high-profile people have been spotted on the app, including actors, musicians, and social media influencers. Some of the celebrities who have been reported to use Raya include Cara Delevingne, Amy Schumer, Kelly Osbourne, Demi Lovato, and Chelsea Handler.

Raya is an exclusive dating app that requires an invitation from a current member and a rigorous application process. While the app is known for its celebrity user base, being famous is not enough to guarantee admission. If you're interested in joining Raya, you'll need to have a creative and accomplished background and be willing to pay the application and membership fees.
