How to Make a First Date Less Awkward


Dating can be an intimidating experience, especially when it's a first date. But there are some simple steps you can take to make the experience more enjoyable and less awkward. Here are five conversation tips to help you make a first date less awkward.

1. Don't Skimp on the Preparation

Before the date, take some time to think about what topics you'd like to discuss. You don't have to come up with a detailed list of questions, but having a few topics in mind can help you stay on track and avoid awkward silences.

2. Plan Something Interactive

If you're feeling particularly nervous, plan an activity that you can do together. This could be anything from going to a museum to playing mini-golf. Having something to do can help take the pressure off of conversation and give you something to focus on.

3. Ask Questions That Evoke Feelings, Passions, and Stories

Rather than asking the typical "get to know you" questions, try to ask questions that will help you get to know your date on a deeper level. Ask questions about their passions, hobbies, and experiences. This will help you get to know them better and make the conversation more interesting.

4. Take Little Risks

It's natural to be a little nervous on a first date, but don't let that stop you from taking a few risks. Don't be afraid to share something personal or ask a bold question. Taking a few risks can help you get to know your date better and make the conversation more interesting.

5. Ask Questions That Matter to You

It's important to ask questions that matter to you. This will help you get to know your date better and make sure you're both on the same page. Don't be afraid to ask questions about your date's values, beliefs, and goals.

Following these tips can help make a first date less awkward and more enjoyable. With a little preparation and some thoughtful conversation, you can make the experience more enjoyable for both of you.
