Is He Love Bombing You? 8 Signs to Look Out For


Love bombing is a form of manipulation used to control someone by showering them with excessive amounts of attention and affection. It is often used to gain control over a person and make them feel obligated to reciprocate the love and attention. If you suspect someone is love bombing you, it is important to be aware of the signs and take steps to protect yourself.

What is Love Bombing?

Love bombing is a form of manipulation that is used to gain control over someone by showering them with excessive amounts of attention and affection. It is often used to make the person feel obligated to reciprocate the love and attention. It can be used in a variety of situations, such as romantic relationships, friendships, and even professional relationships.

Signs of Being Love Bombed

Love bombing can be difficult to spot, as it often starts off as genuine affection. However, there are some tell-tale signs that can help you identify if someone is love bombing you. Here are 8 signs to look out for:

1. They Send You Lavish Gifts: Love bombers will often send you lavish gifts, such as jewelry, clothes, and trips, in a short amount of time. This is a sign that they are trying to buy your affection.

2. They Give Overwhelming Compliments: Love bombers will often give you overwhelming compliments, even before they know enough about you to warrant such a compliment.

3. They Are Overly Attentive: Love bombers will often be overly attentive, constantly texting and calling you, and always wanting to be around you.

4. They Are Quick to Commit: Love bombers will often be quick to commit to a relationship, wanting to move in together or get married after only a few weeks of dating.

5. They Are Possessive: Love bombers will often be possessive, wanting to know where you are and who you are with at all times.

6. They Are Controlling: Love bombers will often be controlling, trying to dictate how you dress, who you hang out with, and what you do.

7. They Are Jealous: Love bombers will often be jealous, accusing you of flirting with other people or being unfaithful.

8. They Are Unreasonable: Love bombers will often be unreasonable, expecting you to drop everything to be with them or do what they want.

Protecting Yourself

If you suspect someone is love bombing you, it is important to take steps to protect yourself. The best way to do this is to set boundaries and stick to them. Make sure you are clear about what you will and won't tolerate, and don't be afraid to walk away if the person is unwilling to respect your boundaries.
