Questions to Build Emotional Intimacy


Creating a strong bond with your partner is essential for a healthy and long-lasting relationship. Intimacy is the key to achieving this, and it can be built through meaningful conversations. Here are questions to help you and your partner get to know each other better and build emotional intimacy.

Questions About Your Relationship

What do you think makes our relationship special? What do you think is the most important thing in a relationship? What do you think our relationship could be doing better? What do you think is the most important thing we should focus on in our relationship? What do you think is the most important thing we should avoid in our relationship?

Questions About Your Past

What have you learned from past relationships? What do you think your younger self would think of our relationship? What have you learned about relationships from your parents? What do you think is the most important lesson you have learned about relationships?

Questions About Your Feelings

When was the last time you felt really loved? When was the last time you felt really appreciated? When was the last time you felt really vulnerable? When was the last time you felt really happy? When was the last time you felt really connected to someone?

Questions About Your Values

What does 'work-life balance' mean to you? What do you think is the most important thing in life? What do you think is the most important thing in a relationship? What do you think is the most important thing to prioritize in life? What do you think is the most important thing to prioritize in a relationship?

Questions About Your Dreams

What are your dreams for the future? What are your dreams for our relationship? What are your dreams for our family? What are your dreams for our career? What are your dreams for our lifestyle?

Questions About Your Vulnerabilities

When was the last time you cried? What do you think is the most difficult thing for you to talk about? What do you think is the most difficult thing for you to open up about? What do you think is the most difficult thing for you to be honest about? What do you think is the most difficult thing for you to share with someone?

These questions can help you and your partner get to know each other better and build emotional intimacy. They can help you understand each other's values, dreams, and vulnerabilities, and create a strong bond. By asking these questions, you can learn more about each other and create a deeper connection.
