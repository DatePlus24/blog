Romance Scams: What Are They After?

Romance scams are a type of fraud that involves a scammer establishing a relationship with a victim, usually through online dating sites or social media, in order to gain their trust and ultimately steal their money. These scams can be incredibly difficult to detect and can have devastating financial and emotional consequences for the victim. So, what exactly do romance scammers want?

Establishing a Relationship

Romance scammers will usually try to establish a relationship with their victim as quickly as possible. They may use flattery and compliments to endear themselves to the victim and build a false sense of trust. The scammer may even propose marriage and make plans to meet in person, but this will never happen.

Gaining Trust

Once the scammer has established a relationship with the victim, they will try to gain their trust. This may involve the scammer asking for personal information, such as banking details or passwords. They may also ask for money to help with a financial emergency or to help them travel to meet the victim.

Stealing Money

Ultimately, the goal of a romance scammer is to steal money from their victim. They may ask for money directly or use the personal information they have gained to access the victim's bank accounts. The scammer may also try to blackmail the victim with threats to reveal personal information or photos.
