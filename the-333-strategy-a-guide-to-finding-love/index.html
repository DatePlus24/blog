What is the 333 Strategy?

The 333 strategy, also known as the "3-4 rule," is a method developed by Dr. Lisa Nobile to help singles find a compatible partner. This strategy requires that singles learn four key principles about their prospect by the end of the third date. Those tenets are chemistry, core values, emotional maturity, and readiness. According to Nobile, this method allows daters to assess chemistry and long-term compatibility.

How Does the 333 Strategy Work?

The 333 strategy is a three-step process that helps singles evaluate their prospects and determine if they are a good match. The first step is to assess chemistry. This involves determining if there is a spark between the two people and if they have similar interests and values. The second step is to evaluate core values. This involves looking at the values and beliefs that the two people have in common. The third step is to assess emotional maturity and readiness. This involves determining if the two people are emotionally mature and ready for a relationship.
