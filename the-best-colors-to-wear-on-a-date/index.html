The Best Colors to Wear on a Date


Dressing up for a date can be quite a task. From choosing the right outfit to deciding the right color, it can be quite overwhelming. While the right color may not guarantee a successful date, it can definitely make a good impression. A survey conducted on the best colors to wear on a date revealed that black was the winner.

Black

Black is a classic color that is sure to make a good impression. It is elegant, sophisticated and versatile. It is also a color that is suitable for any occasion. Moreover, black is a color that looks good on everyone and can be paired with any other color.

Red

Red is a color that is sure to turn heads. It is a color that is associated with passion and romance. It is also a color that is sure to make you stand out. According to the survey, men mostly liked women in red best.

Blue

Blue is a color that is suitable for both men and women on a first date. It is a color that is associated with trust and reliability. It is also a color that can be paired with any other color.

Colors to Avoid

While there are certain colors that are suitable for a date, there are also some colors that should be avoided. The colors to absolutely avoid on a first date are orange and brown. These colors can make you look dull and unappealing.
