What are the Risks of Unprotected Sex?

Having unprotected sex, especially with someone you don't know well, can put you at risk of contracting a sexually transmitted infection (STI). This includes HIV, gonorrhea, chlamydia, and other infections. It can also lead to an unplanned pregnancy.
