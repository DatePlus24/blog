What is an Emotional Affair?

Before delving into the percentage of emotional affairs that turn physical, it is important to understand what an emotional affair is. An emotional affair is an intimate relationship between two people who are not married or in a committed relationship. It is characterized by strong emotional connections, shared secrets, and an intense level of intimacy. This type of relationship is often kept secret and is considered to be inappropriate by society.
