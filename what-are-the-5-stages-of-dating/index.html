What are the 5 Stages of Dating?

Whether you're at the start of a blossoming relationship or been with your significant other for years, every relationship goes through the same five stages of dating. These five stages are attraction, reality, commitment, intimacy and finally, engagement.

Attraction

The first stage of dating is attraction. This is when two people first meet and feel an initial spark of interest in each other. This is usually based on physical attraction, but it can also be based on shared interests, values, and beliefs.

Reality

Commitment

The third stage of dating is commitment. This is when the couple decides to make a commitment to each other and take their relationship to the next level. This is when the couple starts to make plans for the future and think about marriage and other long-term goals.

Intimacy

The fourth stage of dating is intimacy. This is when the couple starts to become more intimate with each other and share more of their thoughts and feelings. This is the stage where the couple starts to build a strong emotional connection and bond.

Engagement

The fifth and final stage of dating is engagement. This is when the couple decides to take their relationship to the next level and get engaged. This is the stage where the couple makes a commitment to each other and starts to plan for a future together.
