What is the average age of Bumble users?


Bumble is a social dating app that allows users to connect with potential partners. It is a popular app among adults of all ages, but the average age of Bumble users is a topic of interest. This article will explore the average age of Bumble users and the usage rate of the app among different age groups.

Usage Rate of Bumble by Age Group

Adults aged 18 to 29 years were most likely to use the social dating app, as 13 percent of respondents from that age group confirmed being current users. Usage rates decreased with age, with 10 percent of adults aged 30 to 44 years using the app, 4 percent of adults aged 45 to 54 years using the app, and 4 percent of adults aged 55 to 64 years using the app. Usage of the app was lowest among adults aged 65 and over, with only 1 percent of respondents in this age group confirming being current users.

Benefits of Bumble for Different Age Groups

The app offers different benefits for users of different ages. Younger users may appreciate the app's emphasis on safety and security, as it requires users to verify their identity and provides them with the ability to block and report other users. Older users may find the app's ease of use appealing, as it has a straightforward and user-friendly interface.

Conclusion

Bumble is a popular social dating app among adults of all ages. The average age of Bumble users is 18 to 29 years, with 13 percent of respondents from this age group confirming being current users.
