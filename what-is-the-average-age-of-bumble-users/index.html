What is the Average Age of Bumble Users?


Bumble is a popular dating app that allows users to match with potential partners. The app has seen a surge in users since its launch in 2014, and its user base continues to grow. But what is the average age of Bumble users?

Usage Rate by Age Group

According to a survey conducted in April 2020, the usage rate of Bumble among adults in the United States is as follows:

• 18-29: 13%
• 30-44: 10%
• 45-54: 4%
• 55-64: 4%

This data suggests that the majority of Bumble users are in the 18-29 age group, followed by the 30-44 age group.

Based on the survey data, the average age of Bumble users is likely to be between 30 and 44 years old. This is consistent with the app's target demographic, which is young adults. While there are users in other age groups, the majority of Bumble users are in the 18-29 and 30-44 age groups.
