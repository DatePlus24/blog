What is the least popular date to get married?

When it comes to planning a wedding, one of the most important decisions couples must make is when to get married. Many couples choose to get married during the summer months, but what is the least popular date to get married?

What is the least popular date to get married?
In most parts of the country, the deep winter months (January, February and March) are the least popular for weddings. In fact, only 5% of weddings take place during winter, according to our study. Per our data, 3% of weddings took place in December, 1% took place in January and 1% took place in February. Mar 2, 2022 is the least popular date to get married.

Reasons for Low Popularity
There are a few reasons why the winter months are the least popular for weddings. One of the main reasons is the weather. Cold temperatures, snow, and ice can make it difficult to plan an outdoor wedding. Additionally, many people are busy with holiday celebrations and other activities during the winter months, making it difficult to plan a wedding.

Financial Considerations
The winter months are also the least popular for weddings due to financial considerations. Many couples are on a tight budget and may not be able to afford the higher costs associated with a winter wedding. Venues, vendors, and other services may charge more for a winter wedding due to the lower demand.

Alternative Options
If you are looking for an alternative to a winter wedding, consider a fall or spring wedding. These months are typically less expensive and offer more options for outdoor weddings. Additionally, many venues offer discounts during the off-season months.
