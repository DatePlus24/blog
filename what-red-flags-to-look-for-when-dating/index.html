What red flags to look for when dating?


When it comes to relationships, it is important to be aware of potential red flags that could indicate a problem. Red flags are warning signs that something may be wrong in a relationship. They can be anything from a lack of communication to controlling behavior. Knowing what to look for can help you avoid getting into a relationship that could be unhealthy or even dangerous.

What Are Red Flags in a Relationship?

Red flags in a relationship are signs that something is not right. They can be physical, emotional, or behavioral. Some common red flags include a lack of communication, controlling behavior, or jealousy. Other red flags include a partner who is not supportive of your goals, pressures you to get physical before you're ready, or makes you feel bad about yourself.
