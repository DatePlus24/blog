What state is best for rent?


Renting a home or apartment is a major decision, and it’s important to know which states offer the best rental opportunities. With the help of data from various sources, we’ve identified the top five states for rent in 2022. 

South Dakota, Wyoming and Montana Take the Lead in 2022 Overall

When it comes to finding the best state for rent in 2022, South Dakota, Wyoming and Montana come out on top. These states offer the most affordable rental options, with a wide range of rental properties available. Additionally, these states have some of the lowest eviction rates in the country, making them attractive options for renters. 

South Dakota has the lowest median rent in the nation, making it a great choice for those looking for an affordable place to live. Wyoming and Montana also offer competitive rental prices, with both states having an average rent that is lower than the national average. 

In terms of availability, South Dakota, Wyoming and Montana have a wide range of rental properties available. These states have a mix of urban and rural rental options, making them ideal for those looking for a variety of rental options. 

Delaware Ranks High in Security

While South Dakota, Wyoming and Montana take the lead in overall rankings, the fifth-place state, Delaware, ranks considerably higher in security. Delaware has some of the lowest eviction rates in the country, as well as laws that benefit tenants. Additionally, Delaware is less prone to natural disasters than other states, making it a safe choice for renters.
