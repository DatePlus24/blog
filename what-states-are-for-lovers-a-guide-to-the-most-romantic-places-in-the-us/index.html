What States are For Lovers? A Guide to the Most Romantic Places in the US

What states are for lovers? This phrase has become synonymous with romance and love, thanks to the iconic slogan “Virginia is for Lovers®” which was created in 1969. For 50 years, this phrase has been used to express the idea that love is found in every corner of the United States. From coast to coast, there are many romantic destinations that are perfect for couples looking to celebrate their love. 

Virginia is For Lovers®

The phrase “Virginia is for Lovers®” was created by the Virginia Tourism Corporation in 1969. It has since become one of the most recognizable and beloved slogans in the world. Virginia is home to many romantic destinations, from the beautiful Blue Ridge Mountains to the stunning beaches of the Outer Banks. There are plenty of romantic activities to enjoy in Virginia, from wine tasting to hot air balloon rides.

Romantic Destinations Across the US

While Virginia may be the most well-known state for lovers, there are plenty of other romantic destinations across the US. Here are some of the most romantic places to visit in the US:

• Hawaii: Hawaii is known for its stunning beaches, lush rainforests, and romantic sunsets. Couples can enjoy a romantic walk on the beach, take a sunset cruise, or explore the island’s many attractions.

• California: California is home to some of the most beautiful beaches in the world. From the rugged coastline of Big Sur to the romantic boardwalks of Santa Cruz, California is the perfect destination for couples looking for a romantic getaway.

• New York: New York City is a great destination for couples looking for a romantic night out. From the iconic skyline to the romantic restaurants and bars, New York City is the perfect place to celebrate love.

• Florida: Florida is home to some of the most beautiful beaches in the world. From the romantic sunsets of the Florida Keys to the white sand beaches of the Gulf Coast, Florida is the perfect destination for couples looking for a romantic escape.

• Maine: Maine is home to some of the most stunning scenery in the US. From the rocky coastline of Acadia National Park to the romantic lighthouses of the Maine coast, Maine is the perfect destination for couples looking for a romantic getaway.

No matter where you go in the US, there are plenty of romantic destinations to explore. From the iconic phrase “Virginia is for Lovers®” to the stunning beaches of Hawaii, there are plenty of romantic places to visit in the US. Whether you’re looking for a romantic getaway or just a romantic night out, there’s a destination for every couple.
