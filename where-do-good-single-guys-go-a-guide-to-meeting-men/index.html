Where Do Good Single Guys Go? A Guide to Meeting Men


Finding a good single guy can be a challenge, but it doesn't have to be. With the right attitude and a bit of creativity, you can find plenty of places and ways to meet single men. From dog parks to cooking classes, here are the top 20 places and ways to meet single men.

Dog Parks

Okay, let me just say this: if you do not have a dog, don't hang out at a dog park. But if you do have a pup, this is a great place to meet single men. Dog parks are a great way to start a conversation with a potential date.

Standing in Line

Do you ever stop to think about how long we spend standing in lines? Whether it's at the grocery store, the bank, or the post office, there are plenty of opportunities to strike up a conversation with a single guy.

On a Plane

If you travel often, you know that planes are a great place to meet people. It's a confined space, so you're forced to talk to the person next to you. Plus, you can use the opportunity to ask for advice on the best places to go in your destination city.

The Gym

The gym is a great place to meet single men. Not only can you strike up a conversation with someone who's working out, but you can also join a class or group fitness session and get to know the other members.

On Public Transportation

Public transportation is another great place to meet single men. Whether you're taking the bus, the train, or the subway, you can strike up a conversation with someone who's also riding.

Cooking Classes

Cooking classes are a great way to meet single men. Not only will you learn a new skill, but you'll also get to know the other people in the class. Plus, you can bond over your shared love of food.

Language Classes

If you're looking to meet single men who are interested in learning a new language, consider taking a language class. You can meet people from all over the world and practice your language skills at the same time.

Networking Events

Networking events are a great way to meet single men who are looking to make connections. Whether it's a business networking event or a singles mixer, you can meet a lot of interesting people in a short amount of time.

These are just a few of the best places and ways to meet single men. With a bit of creativity and an open mind, you can find plenty of opportunities to meet the man of your dreams.
