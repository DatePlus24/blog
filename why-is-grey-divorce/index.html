What is Grey Divorce?

Grey divorce is a term used to describe the divorce of individuals over the age of 50. It is a relatively new phenomenon, as couples in this age group were more likely to stay married in the past. The number of grey divorces has been steadily increasing since the 1990s, with a recent spike in 2020 due to the pandemic.
